Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026 · Last updated: July 1, 2026

Folio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Folio mobile application (the "App"), a personal expense tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Folio, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account information (via Google Sign-In):

  • Email address
  • Display name and profile picture (if provided by Google)
  • Google account identifier (used for authentication only)

Financial data you enter:

  • Income and expense transactions (amount, date, category, notes, merchant name)
  • Custom categories and budgets
  • Preferred currency and display name

Device and app settings (stored locally on your device):

  • Authentication session token (stored in secure device storage)
  • App lock / biometric preference
  • Onboarding and display preferences

We do not collect:

  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, or SMS messages
  • Bank account or credit card numbers
  • Advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync your expense data across devices
  • Display balances, insights, budgets, and exported statements
  • Protect your account with optional app lock
  • Improve app reliability and fix errors

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or selling your information.

3. Third-Party Services

Folio uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Sign-In — for secure authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • MongoDB Atlas — cloud database hosting for your synced account and transaction data. Subject to MongoDB's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — API and website hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your personal or financial data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure cloud servers. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure storage. You may optionally enable biometric or device lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face) to restrict access to the App on your device.

While we implement reasonable safeguards, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request deletion, we will remove your personal data from our servers within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information within the App
  • Export your monthly transaction data as a PDF from the App
  • Sign out and remove the local session from your device at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Folio is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and revise the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
